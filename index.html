About Me

I'm a creative designer with over 7 years of experience specializing in UI/UX design, brand identity, and digital experiences. My design philosophy centers around creating meaningful connections between users and products.

After graduating with a degree in Graphic Design from Central Saint Martins, I've worked with renowned brands and startups alike, helping them elevate their digital presence and create memorable experiences.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me exploring photography, traveling to new places, or attending design workshops to constantly expand my creative horizons.
